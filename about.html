About CarXplore

Your premier destination for premium automobiles

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by Parth and Shubham , CarXplore has become one of the leading car dealerships in the country. We specialize in offering a premium selection of luxury, performance, electric, and hybrid vehicles from the world's top manufacturers.

Our mission is to provide an exceptional car buying experience that goes beyond transactions. We're passionate about helping customers find the perfect vehicle that matches their lifestyle, preferences, and budget.

With a team of automotive enthusiasts and experts, we pride ourselves on our deep knowledge of every vehicle we sell and our commitment to customer satisfaction.
